Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

At Sarthak Mail, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we handle your information when you use our temporary email service. We believe in complete transparency — we don't collect, store, or share any personal data.

What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We don't ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.
  • No IP Addresses: We don't log or store IP addresses. Your network identity remains completely anonymous.
  • No Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any form of persistent storage that could identify you.
  • No Email Content: We don't read, store, or forward your emails. All messages are handled by the mail.tm API and are not persisted by us.
  • No Session Data: We don't track your browsing behavior or session history.

What We Do With Your Temporary Address

When you generate a temporary email address, it is created on the mail.tm service. The address exists only for as long as you need it. Emails sent to your temporary address are stored by mail.tm and retrieved via their API. We act as a middleware — we display the emails in your browser but do not cache, store, or retain any data.

Data Retention

Since we don't store any data, there's nothing to delete. Your temporary email address and its inbox are managed by the mail.tm service, which automatically cleans up old addresses periodically. If you close your browser, your session ends immediately.

Third-Party Services

We use the mail.tm API to provide the core email functionality. Their privacy policy governs how they handle email data. We recommend reviewing their policy for complete transparency. We do not share any data with any other third parties.

Your Rights

  • Right to Anonymity: You can use our service without revealing any personal information.
  • Right to Disconnect: Simply close your browser or generate a new address — no traces remain.
  • Right to Data Erasure: Since we don't store data, your privacy is automatically guaranteed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
